Corporate Strategy

Defines the portfolio of businesses and how they relate to each other.

Description

Multi-level decisions about which businesses/markets the organization will compete in (Diversification, Concentration, Vertical Integration, etc.). For you: Should we stay SIMRS-only or expand into adjacent healthcare segments?

Strategic Options for SIMRS

Related Diversification (Recommended)

Expand into complementary healthcare IT (pharmacy systems, clinic management, insurance platforms)

Unrelated Diversification (Risky)

Expand into non-healthcare software (not recommended given your expertise)

Vertical Integration

Build or acquire EHR data analytics, patient engagement layers

Concentration

Deepen SIMRS dominance in hospital segment only (defensive)
