## Relationship to Your Context (SIMRS Diversification)

### How Dynamic Capabilities Enables Your Growth
1. **Sensing** – Identifying new healthcare opportunities (telemedicine, patient records management, clinical decision support, pharmacy networks, insurance integration via VClaim)
2. **Seizing** – Building/acquiring capabilities to enter new markets
3. **Transforming** – Reconfiguring SIMRS core into a modular platform serving multiple healthcare segments

### How Ambidexterity Works for You
- **Exploit:** Continue optimizing SIMRS (Lean + Scrum in operations)
- **Explore:** Experiment with new healthcare products (Blue Ocean + Business Model Innovation)
- **Balance:** Organizational ambidexterity keeps both moving without cannibalizing resources

### Where Lean & Scrum Live
- **Scrum** = Operational execution layer (Sprint planning, iterative delivery)
- **Lean** = Process optimization layer (waste elimination, continuous improvement)
- Both feed into "Execution & Process Excellence" but don't drive strategy—they enable it

### Your Psychological Safety Work
- Part of "Organizational Culture" layer
- Foundation for effective change management and ambidexterity
- Enables honest feedback loops in Scrum and continuous improvement in Lean
