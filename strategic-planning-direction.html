STRATEGIC PLANNING & DIRECTION

Strategic Planning & Direction is the process of defining organizational vision, mission, and long-term strategic direction. It establishes the roadmap for where the organization is headed and what it aims to achieve within a defined timeframe.

Why It Matters for SIMRS

For a healthcare software company expanding into new segments (diversification), strategic planning provides the essential guardrails:

  • Core Purpose: Clarifies the "Why" (e.g., Empower healthcare delivery across Southeast Asia).
  • Long-term Vision: Establishing a persistent 5-10 year horizon for growth.
  • Portfolio Priorities: Deciding which segments (clinics, labs, pharmacies) to enter and in what sequence.
