Strategic Planning & Direction

The process of defining organizational vision, mission, and long-term strategic direction. Establishes the roadmap for where the organization is headed and what it aims to achieve within a defined timeframe.

Why It Matters for SIMRS

For a healthcare software company expanding into new segments, strategic planning clarifies:

  • Core purpose: "Empower healthcare delivery across Southeast Asia"
  • Long-term vision: Establishing a 5-10 year horizon for growth and dominance.
  • Portfolio priorities: Deciding which healthcare segments (clinics, labs, pharmacies) to enter and when.
